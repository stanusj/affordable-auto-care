Our Services

Everything With a Motor. One Shop.

Cars, trucks, RVs, and commercial equipment — full-service repair, maintenance, and smog testing for Dixon and Solano County.

Check-engine lights, drivability problems, and hard-to-find issues. We diagnose before we quote, so you're not paying to guess.

Wheel Bearings, Suspension & Steering
