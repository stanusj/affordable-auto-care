Our Story

Dixon's Cost-Effective Alternative to the Dealership

Affordable Auto Care is a full-service auto repair and preventive maintenance center that's been doing business in Dixon, California for over 30 years. We're family owned and operated, and we built this shop to be the honest, cost-effective alternative to dealership service — for personal cars, work trucks, and everything in between.

Customers tell us again and again that what keeps them coming back isn't just the price — it's that we tell them the truth about what's actually wrong, and we fix it right the first time.
